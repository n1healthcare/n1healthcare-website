Privacy Policy

At N1 Healthcare Ltd., we deeply value the trust you place in us with your personal and health-related information. Our commitment to protecting your privacy forms the cornerstone of our relationship with you. This Comprehensive Privacy Policy outlines the manner in which we handle, use, and safeguard your information when you engage with our website and services.

Scope and Consent

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through your interactions with our website and services. By accessing our website and utilizing our services, you consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect
  1. Personal Identification Information: When you visit our website or use our services, we collect personal information that you voluntarily provide. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, contact details, and any other identifiers that you choose to share with us.
  2. Health and Medical Data: Central to our services, we collect a wide range of health and medical data. This includes detailed medical histories, genetic information, results from medical tests and procedures, and any other health-related information pertinent to crafting your personalized health report.
  3. Technical and Usage Data: Our website automatically gathers certain technical information. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, and other usage data related to your interactions with our website, such as page views and navigation patterns.
The information we collect is utilized in various ways:
  • With medical professionals and specialists, as required, for the provision of personalized health services and consultations.
  • With third-party service providers who perform various functions on our behalf, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • With legal and regulatory authorities, in compliance with legal obligations and to protect against fraud or other illegal activities.

We are firmly committed to not selling, trading, or otherwise transferring your personally identifiable information to external parties without your explicit consent, except in the scenarios outlined above.

Data Protection and Security

We employ state-of-the-art security measures to safeguard your personal information. This includes robust encryption technologies, secure server infrastructure, and strict access control protocols, ensuring that your data is protected against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Your Privacy Rights

You retain full rights over your personal information. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Please contact us directly for any such requests, and we will act promptly to accommodate your needs.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or relevant laws. Any amendments will be posted on our website, and we encourage you to review the Privacy Policy periodically.
