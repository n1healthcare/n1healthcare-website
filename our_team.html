Our Team

At N1 Healthcare Ltd., we take immense pride in our team, comprising an assemblage of skilled technical writers and clinicians, each contributing their unique expertise to the creation of our comprehensive health reports. The profiles presented here exemplify just a fraction of our extensive team, which includes several dozen more specialists, each playing a pivotal role in our commitment to personalized healthcare.

Clinicians

Dr. Glenn H. Bock

Pediatrics and Nephrology

Dr. Bock worked as an academic clinician and physician-scientist for nearly 40 years. He offers a great deal of knowledge that includes both hereditary and acquired kidney diseases, transplantation medicine, renal replacement therapies and a broad understanding of immunology, autoimmune diseases, nutrition, longevity, and gene therapies. He has authored more than 40 peer-reviewed papers and has presented his research at national and international scientific symposia. He has mentored numerous subspecialty fellows, many of whom hold senior leadership positions at well-regarded institutions throughout the US.

Craig Napolitano,

MD, FACC, Cardiologist

Dr. Napolitano is a board-certified cardiologist and lipidologist specializing in preventive cardiology. He is one of the few doctors worldwide holding seven simultaneous board certifications, which include cardiology, internal medicine, lipidology, echocardiography, nuclear cardiology, cardiac CT, and vascular ultrasound. As one of approximately 700 board-certified lipidologists in the United States, he brings additional expertise in novel treatments that may not be widely known among other healthcare providers.

Miriam Galvonas Jasiulionis,

PhD

Dr. Jasiulionis is a scientific researcher and professor dedicated to studying the relationship between cellular stress, epigenetic alterations and disease onset, and genetic and epigenetic changes, especially in oncology. She has more than 60 scientific articles, a book and several book chapters, and is a co-founder of the IAssist startup.

Scott M. Weissman,

MS, CGC, Genetic Counselor

Scott received his Bachelor of Science degree in Biology from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 1998. After graduation, he spent two years working in a clinical genetic testing laboratory at the University of Illinois at Chicago performing testing to monitor minimal residual disease in individuals affected by leukemia or lymphoma. In 2002, he graduated from Northwestern University in Chicago with his Masters of Science in Genetic Counseling. He has been board certified in genetic counseling by the American Board of Genetic Counseling since 2002. His primary focus is hereditary cancer syndromes but also worked with individuals who had hereditary cardiac disease, neurologic conditions as well as a multitude of other adult-onset genetic conditions.

Dr. Heather Moday,

MD, Integrative Medicine Physician

Dr. Moday is a board-certified specialist in Integrative Medicine, as well as an Allergy and Immunology. She earned her medical degree from Tulane University in New Orleans and went on to complete her residency and fellowship in Internal Medicine and Allergy and Immunology at Montefiore Hospital/ Albert Einstein Medical Center in New York City. After a decade in private practice, Dr. Moday pursued further training, completing a fellowship in Integrative Medicine at the University of Arizona. She opened her private Functional and Integrative Medical practice in Philadelphia in 2015.

Professor Lakshminarayan Ranganath

Metabolic medicine

Professor Lakshminarayan Ranganath, a consultant at the Royal Liverpool University Hospital, UK, is a prominent figure in metabolic medicine. He founded the NHS-funded National Alkaptonuria Centre (NAC) and serves as its inaugural Director. Prof Ranganath's notable achievements include conducting a national survey identifying AKU patients globally, pioneering AKU patient assessments, and leading DevelopAKUre, an EU-funded program involving a major nitisinone clinical trial. He has authored over 200 peer-reviewed papers and mentored numerous research students.
