Frequently Asked Questions

At N1 Healthcare Ltd., we understand that you may have questions about our services, the details of our health reports, and our billing model. Below, we have compiled and answered some of the most frequently asked questions to provide you with comprehensive insights into our approach and practices

What Services Does N1 Healthcare Ltd. Offer?

N1 Healthcare Ltd. specializes in creating personalized, comprehensive health reports for our clients. These reports cover a wide range of health aspects, including family medical history, DNA analysis, lifestyle analysis, comprehensive medical history, and a thorough review of medications and supplements. We also offer ongoing updates to these reports as new health data becomes available.

How Detailed are the Comprehensive Health Reports?

Our health reports are exceptionally detailed, often spanning up to 1000 pages. They include an exhaustive analysis of your health, incorporating past and present medical tests, genetic data, lifestyle factors, and more. Each report is tailored to the individual, ensuring a personalized health analysis that evolves over time.

Who are our target clients?

Yes, our services are accessible to clients globally. N1 Healthcare Ltd. is committed to providing comprehensive health research and information services to a diverse international clientele. Regardless of your location, you can benefit from our in-depth health reports and personalized healthcare insights. We leverage technology and digital communication platforms to ensure our clients, wherever they may be around the world, receive the same high standard of service and access to the latest in healthcare information

How Is the Hourly Billing Model Structured?

Our billing model is based on an hourly rate, which varies depending on the expertise required for different sections of your health report. This model allows for flexibility and transparency. Before starting, we provide an estimated range of hours required to complete your report, ensuring clarity and predictability in billing

Can I Request Additional Tests or Analyses?

Yes, you can request additional tests or analyses. Our health reports are dynamic, and we can incorporate new medical tests as they become available. We also may recommend additional tests if we believe they are necessary for a more comprehensive health overview

Are Recommendations for Specialists and Clinics Included in the Health Reports?

Yes, we provide recommendations for specialists and clinics as part of our health reports. These recommendations are based on your specific health needs and our extensive network of leading healthcare professionals and institutions around the globe

How often are the Health Reports Updated?

Our health reports are updated regularly to reflect the latest medical findings and any new health data specific to your profile. The frequency of updates depends on changes in your health status and the emergence of new medical information relevant to your health

What If I Have Concerns About My Health Report?

If you have any concerns or questions about your health report, we encourage you to contact us. Our team is committed to ensuring your complete understanding and satisfaction with the information provided in your report.

Absolutely. We adhere to strict confidentiality and data protection protocols to ensure that your health data is secure and private. We understand the sensitive nature of medical information and are committed to upholding the highest standards of data privacy.

What is the scope of services provided by N1 Healthcare Ltd.?

The name 'N1 Healthcare' is inspired by the concept of 'n-of-1 trials' in medicine. N-of-1 trials are highly personalized clinical trials where a single patient forms the entire trial. This approach allows for the tailored assessment of how effective a specific treatment or intervention is for an individual, as opposed to a larger group. The benefits of n-of-1 trials include a deep focus on personalized medicine, where treatments are customized based on the individual's unique health profile, genetics, and specific responses to treatments. By adopting this philosophy, N1 Healthcare underscores its commitment to providing personalized healthcare insights and solutions, ensuring that our services are as individualized and relevant to each client as possible. We believe in the power of personalized healthcare to significantly improve health outcomes by focusing on the unique needs and conditions of each client.
